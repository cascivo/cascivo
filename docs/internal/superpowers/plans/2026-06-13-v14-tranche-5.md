**Goal:** Complement automated checks with real assistive-technology results and map cascade
to the legal frameworks customers are bound by — stating plainly what automation can't catch
and what depends on the consuming application.

**Architecture:** A documented manual AT-testing methodology + a representative results matrix
in `docs/specs/at-matrix.md`, surfaced on an accessibility-statement page in the landing a11y
section. The statement maps EAA / EN 301 549 / Section 508 to cascade's verified conformance
and links the axe results + AT matrix.

**Tech stack:** unchanged. AT testing is manual (NVDA/JAWS/VoiceOver) — not automated in CI
(deferred by the roadmap).

## Tranche exit criteria

1. `docs/specs/at-methodology.md` documents the AT stacks, per-component test steps, and the
   representative set (explicitly representative, not exhaustive).
2. `docs/specs/at-matrix.md` records real results per (component × stack), with `not tested`
   used honestly where a stack couldn't be run; clear AT bugs surfaced are fixed + re-tested.
3. The landing a11y page states the axe coverage ceiling (automation catches a minority).
4. An accessibility-statement page maps EAA / EN 301 549 / Section 508, summarizes WCAG 2.2 AA
   - APG conformance from live data, links the AT matrix + axe results, and scopes claims to
     component-level conformance under correct usage (no implied product compliance).
5. Root gate + drift check exit 0.
