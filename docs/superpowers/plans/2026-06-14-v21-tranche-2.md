**Goal:** A functional **Cascade Pay** dashboard: a revenue overview (AreaChart + KPI row), payment
volume by product (BarChart), a paginated transactions/customers `DataTable` with status badges, a date
range filter that re-queries the mock-api, and an optimistic "refund" mutation that persists across
reload and toasts.

**Architecture:** New `apps/examples/pay/` mirroring `apps/examples/deploy/` (vite config + aliases incl.
`@cascivo/example-kit` and `@cascivo/charts`, `data/`, `api/`, `App.tsx`, `sections/`, `test/`). No kit
changes expected; if the kit needs a tweak, do it minimally and note it.

**Tech stack:** unchanged. `useSignals()` first; `useSignalEffect`/`useSimulation` for any timers; no
`useState`/`useEffect`/`useContext`. Strings via `@cascivo/i18n`. Charts fed by signals.
