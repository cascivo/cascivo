# v20 Tranche 4 — Overlays & navigation (highest risk)

> **For agentic workers:** REQUIRED SUB-SKILL: superpowers:subagent-driven-development or
> superpowers:executing-plans. Steps use checkbox (`- [ ]`) syntax. **This is the highest-risk tranche** —
> overlays carry focus traps, scroll-lock, and Esc/scrim behavior that must not regress.
>
> Give the overlay and navigation components real mobile patterns: Modal becomes a bottom-sheet/fullscreen
> below `md`; Dropdown/Select menus are capped to the viewport so they never overflow; Tabs scroll
> horizontally instead of wrapping awkwardly. All additive below the canonical breakpoints — desktop
> overlay behavior is frozen.
>
> Current-state assumptions — RE-VERIFY before starting:
>
> - `modal.module.css`: `--_modal-width: 24/32/48rem`, `width: var(--_modal-width)`,
>   `max-width: calc(100vw - var(--cascivo-space-8))` (~line 71); **no** `@media`, no sheet behavior.
> - `dropdown.module.css:11` `min-inline-size: 10rem`; anchored positioning via `@supports (anchor-name)`.
> - `tabs.module.css`: flex + wrap; no horizontal scroll.
> - Modal/dialog behavior (focus trap, Esc, scrim, scroll-lock) is signal-driven — re-read the TSX to
>   confirm it uses `useSignalEffect` (NOT `useEffect`) for the `<dialog>`/listener side effects. Do not
>   convert any of it to `useEffect`.
> - `alert-dialog`, `select` (menu portion), `toast` are siblings to check.

**Goal:** Below `md` (40rem), Modal/AlertDialog render as a bottom-sheet or fullscreen sheet pinned to the
viewport edge; Dropdown/Select menus never exceed the viewport inline-size (no horizontal scroll at
320px); Tabs scroll horizontally with a usable affordance. Focus trap, Esc/scrim close, and scroll-lock
are unchanged. All enrolled in the sweep.

**Architecture:** CSS-first changes in `modal`, `alert-dialog`, `dropdown`, `select`, `tabs`, `toast`
modules; TSX touched only if a sheet needs a `data-` hook (no logic/hook changes). Use canonical `md` in
any `@media`.

**Tech stack:** unchanged. CSS-first; if any TSX side effect is touched it stays `useSignalEffect`.
